🐣 My Pet

👆 Click the egg to hatch!

Hunger: 100

Happiness: 100

Energy: 100

Points: 0

How To Play

Feed your angel to keep hunger high.

- Candy gives 5 hunger and 3 happiness.

- Pie gives 7 hunger.

- Apple gives 10 hunger and 1 happiness.

- Baby bottle gives 16 hunger and 5 energy.

Play the star game to increase happiness. Click the stars to make it go away. +25 happiness.

Earn points from playing the minigame and buy food. The numbers beside the food represents how much it is.

Let your angel sleep to recover energy. If accidentally pressed, click wake up. It needs fully 10 seconds of sleep to get 30 energy.

Try to keep all stats above 0!
